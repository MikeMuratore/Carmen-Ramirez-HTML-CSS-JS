Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Will?

A will is a legal document that coordinates the distribution of your assets after death and can appoint guardians for minor children

What is a Trust?

A trust is a fiduciary arrangement that allows a third party, or trustee, to hold assets on behalf of a beneficiary or beneficiaries.

Will the State take away my property if I dont leave a Will?

No.

How big is the team on your Law Firm?

Our firm is a small, focused team of attorneys and staff who work collaboratively to provide attentive, high-quality legal representation to our clients.

How long will it take to get my Will or Trust done?

The process is usually less than 30 days.  Carmen Ramirez limits the number of new clients she takes on in a month, so that she can ensure that each of her clients gets the time and attention it takes to understand their plans, write their document, and meet with them to sign.  Appointments are set for one hour
